About Our Firm

The Law Offices of John A. Douglas provides sophisticated legal representation for real estate transactions, creditor's rights matters, and mortgage lending law. With a dedicated focus on these specialized areas, we offer the expertise and personal attention that our clients deserve.

Our Approach

We understand that every client's situation is unique. That's why we take the time to thoroughly understand your specific needs and goals before developing a tailored legal strategy.

Our firm is committed to providing prompt, professional, and personalized legal services that achieve optimal results in a cost-effective manner.

With admissions to both the Texas State Bar and the Federal Court for the Northern District of Texas, we can represent clients in a wide range of jurisdictions and legal matters. Our extensive experience in real estate law, creditor's rights, and mortgage lending gives us the perspective needed to effectively fight for your rights.

Our Practice Areas

We offer specialized legal services in the following areas to meet your specific needs.

Real Estate Law

At the Law Offices of John A. Douglas, we provide comprehensive legal services for all your real estate needs. Whether you're buying or selling property, negotiating leases, or dealing with complex real estate disputes, our experienced team is here to protect your interests.

Real estate transactions involve significant financial investments and complex legal requirements. Attorney Douglas has successfully handled numerous real estate matters for individuals, investors, and businesses throughout Texas.

Our real estate practice encompasses various areas, including but not limited to:

Residential Real Estate

We handle all aspects of residential real estate transactions:

  • Purchase and sale agreements
  • Title examinations and insurance
  • Mortgage financing
  • Property disputes
  • Homeowner association matters

Commercial Real Estate

We support businesses with their commercial property needs:

  • Commercial property transactions
  • Commercial leasing
  • Development projects
  • Land use and zoning issues
  • Commercial property disputes

Landlord-Tenant Law

We represent both landlords and tenants in various matters:

  • Lease drafting and review
  • Eviction proceedings
  • Lease violations
  • Security deposit disputes
  • Property maintenance issues

Real Estate Litigation

We resolve disputes involving real property:

  • Boundary disputes
  • Easement and right-of-way issues
  • Title defects
  • Real estate contract disputes
  • Construction defects

Creditor's Rights

The Law Offices of John A. Douglas is committed to protecting the rights of creditors and helping them recover amounts owed through effective legal strategies. We understand the challenges creditors face when attempting to collect debts and enforce their rights.

Attorney Douglas has extensive experience representing banks, financial institutions, businesses, and individual creditors in various debt collection and creditor's rights matters. We take pride in our ability to navigate complex legal processes to help our clients achieve their collection goals.

Our creditor's rights practice focuses on various aspects of debt collection and enforcement, including:

Debt Collection

We pursue comprehensive debt collection services:

  • Commercial debt collection
  • Consumer debt recovery
  • Judgment enforcement
  • Asset investigation
  • Settlement negotiations

Foreclosure Proceedings

We help creditors with secured interests in property:

  • Residential foreclosures
  • Commercial foreclosures
  • Judicial and non-judicial foreclosures
  • Deed in lieu of foreclosure
  • Post-foreclosure matters

Bankruptcy Creditor Representation

We protect creditors' interests in bankruptcy proceedings:

  • Proof of claim filings
  • Relief from automatic stay
  • Objections to discharge
  • Preference and fraudulent transfer defense
  • Chapter 7, 11, and 13 proceedings

Lien Enforcement

We help creditors enforce various types of liens:

  • Mechanic's and materialman's liens
  • Judgment liens
  • Tax liens
  • Landlord's liens
  • Garnishment proceedings

Mortgage Lending Law

At the Law Offices of John A. Douglas, we provide comprehensive legal representation in all aspects of mortgage lending law. We advise lenders, borrowers, and other participants in the mortgage industry on complex legal matters related to loan origination, servicing, and enforcement.

Mortgage lending involves intricate regulations and requirements at both state and federal levels. Attorney Douglas has extensive experience in navigating these complex legal frameworks to help clients achieve their goals while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Our mortgage lending practice encompasses a wide range of services, including:

Loan Documentation

We assist with all aspects of mortgage loan documentation:

  • Promissory notes
  • Mortgages and deeds of trust
  • Security agreements
  • Closing documents
  • Loan modifications

Regulatory Compliance

We help navigate the complex regulatory landscape:

  • Truth in Lending Act (TILA)
  • Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA)
  • Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)
  • Fair Housing Act
  • State lending and licensing requirements

Default Servicing

We provide legal guidance for managing defaulted loans:

  • Default notices
  • Foreclosure proceedings
  • Loss mitigation options
  • Deed in lieu of foreclosure
  • Short sales

Secondary Market Transactions

We support mortgage industry participants in secondary market activities:

  • Loan purchases and sales
  • Securitization
  • Due diligence reviews
  • Investor requirements
  • Compliance with GSE guidelines
